Subpart 1845.3—Authorizing the Use and Rental of Government Property

Source: 76 FR 2005, Jan. 12, 2011, unless otherwise noted.

Parent topic: PART 1845—GOVERNMENT PROPERTY

1845.302 Use of Government property on contracts with foreign governments or international organizations.

(a) NASA contracting officers will recover a fair share of the cost of Government property if such property is used in performing services or manufacturing articles for foreign countries or for international organizations.
