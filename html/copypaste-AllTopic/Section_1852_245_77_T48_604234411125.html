1852.245-77 List of Government property furnished pursuant to FAR 52.245-2.

As prescribed in 1845.107-70(h), insert the following clause:

List of Government Property Furnished Pursuant to FAR 52.245-2 (JAN 2011)

For performance of work under this contract, the Government will make available Government property identified below or in Attachment ____ [Insert attachment number or “not applicable”] of this contract on a nocharge-for-use basis pursuant to FAR 52.245-2, Government Property Installation Operation Services, as incorporated in this contract. The Contractor shall use this property in the performance of this contract at ____ [Insert applicable site(s) where property will be used] and at other location(s) as may be approved by the Contracting Officer.

[Insert a description of the item(s), acquisition date, quantity, acquisition cost, and applicable equipment information]

(End of clause)
